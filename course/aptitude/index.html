FAQ

  • Why Is this not a certification course?

    Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, etc are not specialization courses. It is a generic requirement which everyone should master and possess.

  • Will my money refunded if I don’t like this course?

    Yes, you can opt for full refund within the first day of the course. After the first day, no refund is possible. Having said that, we are confident that it will never come to that.

  • Will 1:1 resume building be done?

    Ideal resume formats will be discussed in the class and all individual doubts will be clarified on it. But, resume has to built by you based on sample formats disucssed in the class. A review can be done after that by expereinced trainers.

  • What are the pre-requisites for the mock drills?

    Everyone needs to get their resume made before mock drills. Also, you should be willing to actively participate when any one is called upon to take the interview before the audience.

  • I am already good with Aptitude. Do you think I should enrol for this course?

    Aptitude and Quants are just one side of the coin in this course. The other part of online tests, resume building, online videos and mock drills are the prime and important contents of this course and is something which most students are unaware of.

  • Can I enroll for other Saarthi Career courses as well?

    Yes you can definitely. However, you have to ensure yourself that you have not enrolled for different batches on same days.
