FAQ

  • Why isn't this a certification course?

    Aptitude, logical reasoning etc are not specialization courses. They are generic requirements that must be worked on at all times.

  • Can I ask for a refund if I don't like this course?

    A complete refund may be demanded within the first day of the course. Amount is non refundable after the first day.

  • Will I prepare my resume with an expert personally?

    The resume will need to be prepared by the student. Ideal resume formats will be discussed in class and all individual doubts will be clarified. The trainers may be consulted for review.

  • What are the pre requisites for mock drills?

    A resume needs to be prepared for a mock drill.

    Active participation is requested when called upon to take interviews before the audience.

  • I am already good at aptitude. Should I still enrol for this course?

    Aptitude and quants make one side of the coin. Online tests, visual solutions and resume building make the other side of it that most students are unaware of.

  • Can I enroll for other Saarthi Career courses as well?

    Yes, as long as timings of the courses do not overlap.
