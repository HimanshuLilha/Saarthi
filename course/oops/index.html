FAQ

  • What is this audit level certificate and Graded Certificate?

    Audit Certificate or Participation Certification will be provided to everyone who enroll for this course. Graded Level Certificate is going to be based on a test or Case Study solving. While Audit Certificate is enough to show that you have learnt a new language to the interview panel, graded certificate will allow you to showcase your superior grasp over the language. You can choose whether you want to take a graded test or not.

  • What are the prerequisites for this course?

    Any one who wants to learn C++ and OOPS concept end to end can take this course. However, you need to know basic syntax of any one programming language just like for, while loops, if statements.

  • Is it online or classroom based training?

    It is instructor led classroom based training program. The instructor's profile are mentioned above and will be present full time as part of the class.

  • How many students will be accomodated in a batch?

    The batch size will be 60 students. We believe in a lot of 1:1 interaction and hence we have kept a batch size which is ideal enough for trainer and student interaction.

  • Is having a laptop compulsory for enrolling for this course?

    No, laptop is not copulsory. However, since there are lab sessions at end of every class, having laptops will allow you to do hands-on and practise what has been done that day in the class.

  • Will my money returned if I don't like the course or teaching skill?

    We have vey expereinced trainers and 5+ of years of experience in training behind us so we are very confident of our quality and mode of teaching. However, if for any reason you find it unsatisfactory, you can ask for complete refund within the first day of attending the course.

  • Will I get some notes at the end of the course for revising the concepts?

    Yes, you will be provided with notes from our trainers at the end of the course for practice and learning.

  • Will you be solving a complete Case Study in the class?

    We will be providing you the complete elaborate content and a case file. You will have to convert that into a project and our team will evaluate it. However, our assistance and help will be provided for completing the case study.
